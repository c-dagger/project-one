About Me

Fun

Camping

My family and I have gone camping since I was a little girl. Over the years, we have evolved from borrowed tents, to our own tents, to a pop-up trailer, to a stationary trailer, to what we have now - an RV trailer. My parents are avid campers so I have always cherished the idea of family time outdoors, with limited cell phone connection. There is no better feeling than listening to 80s rock, making smores in your favorite hoodie, going on a late night golf cart ride by the beach, and cracking jokes with your family by the campfire.

Travelling

Travelling is one of the greatest joys of my life. I try to go to at least one destination vacation a year. I love travelling because it is a reality check that bursts our every day bubble. It is a notion that there is so much more to life than just our immediate surroundings, our every day stressors. It is hope and courage that you can always change your life and move in a new direction, literally. Travelling teaches us to be humble and grateful for what we have and to remind us that life is too short to stress on the little things.
